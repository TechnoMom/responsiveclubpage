About

We're a diverse group of people with just one thing in common: we love to read. We come together once a month to discuss what we've read lately and compare notes on a book that's chosen by the membership. Other events include trips to the Decatur Book Fair, author signings, and the like. We meet at various branches of the Fulton County Public Library every month.

News

We had quite a turnout in January for our discussion of Michelle Obama's Becoming. This month's book is What We Don't Talk About When We Talk About Fat by Aubrey Gordon, better known as Your Fat Friend.
